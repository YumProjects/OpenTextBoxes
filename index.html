Open Text Boxes Created by Ben Hess Welcome to Open Text Boxes, the infinite multiplayer digital graffiti wall.

How it works:
  • Anyone can post short messages or view other's messages on the infinate wall.
  • When you post somthing, it becomes visible to everyone who visits that location.
  • You can veiw your current location in the top right corner.
Controls:
  • Drag mouse to move around the wall.
  • Click the "Add New Post" button in the bottom left to add a new post to the wall.
